## The Docs-as-System Manifesto

> Docs-as-System is a live development methodology in which documentation, code,  
> and an agent integrated into the IDE operate together as one synchronized system.

A truly intelligent system is not the one that works alone,  
but the one that remains understandable long after everyone has gone home.
